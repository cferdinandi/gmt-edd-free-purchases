# GMT EDD Free Purchases
Let people purchase products through EDD just by supplying their email address.

[Download GMT EDD Free Purchases](https://github.com/cferdinandi/gmt-edd-free-purchases/archive/master.zip)



## Getting Started

Getting started with EDD Free Purchases is as simple as installing a plugin:

1. Upload the `gmt-edd-free-purchases` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the Plugins menu in WordPress.
3. Configure your settings under `Free Purchase` on the download page.
4. Add the included shortcode anywhere on your site to include an email signup field to authorize the purchase.

And that's it, you're done. Nice work!

It's recommended that you also install the [GitHub Updater plugin](https://github.com/afragen/github-updater) to get automatic updates.
